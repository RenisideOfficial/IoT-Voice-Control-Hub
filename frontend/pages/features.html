Remote Control

With Alpha Control Hub, you can turn appliances ON or OFF instantly from any device. Whether you're at home or away, control your devices in real-time with the web dashboard or mobile app.

Supported devices include lights, fans, air conditioners, smart plugs, and other IoT-enabled appliances. The system provides status feedback so you always know whether a device is active or idle.

Voice Commands

Control your appliances hands-free using natural voice commands. Alpha integrates seamlessly with Alexa and Google Assistant, allowing you to issue commands like "Turn on the living room lights" or "Set the thermostat to 22Â°C".

Custom voice phrases can be added for a more personalized experience.

Scheduling & Timers

Automate your devices with daily, weekly, or custom schedules. You can set lights to turn on at sunset, or have the coffee maker start brewing in the morning automatically.

The scheduling engine is flexible and allows you to combine conditions, timers, and recurring events for full automation.

Custom Feedback

Receive notifications and audible responses from your devices. Alpha supports Text-to-Speech feedback, letting devices announce their status or alert you about completed tasks.

Multi-Device Support

Manage multiple devices from a single interface. Group appliances by room, function, or user, and control them collectively or individually.

Secure & Cross-Platform

Alpha ensures your devices and data are protected with encrypted logins and secure device pairing. The platform works across Android, iOS, and web, so you can control your home from anywhere.
